Featured Projects
Beautykaufhaus - Webshop

For BeautyKaufhaus.de, a beauty and cosmetics retailer, I enhanced the webshop by combining user experience design with strategic functionality. I developed a dynamic category structure to make product discovery effortless, and integrated Trusted Shops for credibility and Google Analytics for actionable insights. The result is a smooth, engaging shopping experience that empowers the owner with powerful administrative and marketing tools.

Smokkers - Webshop

For Smokkers.de, an online shop specializing in vapes, hookahs, and tobacco products, I focused on both functionality and compliance. I ensured the entire webshop met legal requirements for tobacco sales, including robust age verification and content restrictions. In addition to technical improvements, I provided copywriting support to refine product descriptions and align the brand’s tone of voice. To support international growth, I developed a custom translation tool that automatically converts product data into multiple languages—significantly expanding reach while reducing the time and cost of manual translations.

Autoglaswelt - Webshop

For Autoglaswelt.de, I set up a modern, conversion-focused webshop specializing in automotive glass. The project included creating a clean, corporate-style theme for the shop as well as a sales funnel to guide users effectively from landing to checkout. I also implemented a custom shop plugin that allows customers to instantly filter through 6,673 different front windshields for a wide range of car models. The result is a fast, intuitive shopping experience optimized for both usability and performance.

Star Piercing - Webshop

For Star-Piercing.com, an online piercing and jewelry store, I contributed to the refinement of their e-commerce platform by focusing on document design. I created cohesive and visually appealing templates for offers, invoices, and order confirmations, ensuring they aligned with the shop's branding and aesthetic. The result is a polished customer experience that maintains the brand's look and feel across every stage of communication.
